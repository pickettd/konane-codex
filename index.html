How to Play

  1. Black removes a center or corner stone, then White removes an adjacent stone.
  2. Select one of your stones with a legal capture to begin a turn.
  3. Jump over opponent stones into empty spaces to capture.
  4. Continue jumping in a straight or turning path while captures remain, or click your stone to end the chain.
  5. If you have no legal move, you lose.
